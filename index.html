My dearest Aeni

There are so many cute things about you that stay in my mind all day.

Your anger is probably my favorite one. Not because you're angry, but because even when you're upset, you still look adorable. The way you argue, the way you say things when you're annoyed, and the way you try to act serious always ends up making me smile.

I love the way you talk too. Even your random messages make my day better. Sometimes I read our chats again just because I miss talking to you.

But there is one thing that always makes me sad. You're my girlfriend, yet I barely get to see your beautiful face. I wish you'd send me more pictures so I can admire the person I care about. And I wish you'd send me voice notes sometimes too. I want to hear your cute voice, your laugh, your little reactions, and the way you say my name.

I love how mysterious you are, how you make me curious about you every day, and how you can make my mood better without even trying. Even when you're stubborn, dramatic, or pretending not to care, I still find you incredibly cute.

Maybe you don't realize it, but the smallest things about you stay in my head for hours. Your messages, your attitude, your jokes, your anger, your kindness — everything.

So yes, I complain about not getting pictures or voice notes, but it's only because I like you so much and I want to know more about the girl who somehow became my favorite person.

with all my love, Yours ♡

page two

Still learning how to love you

Maybe the problem isn't that I don't care enough. Maybe it's that I don't always know how to show it.

The truth is, I don't have much experience when it comes to loving someone. I don't always know what makes you smile, what words you need to hear, or what little things make your day better. Sometimes I stay quiet when I should say more, and sometimes I fail to understand what you're trying to tell me.

It's not because you're unimportant to me. It's because I'm still learning.

There are moments when I feel like I'm not good enough for you. Not because you make me feel that way, but because I see all the things I wish I could do better. I wish I understood you more, made you happier, and gave you fewer reasons to be upset.

But despite all that, one thing is real: every day I try. I pay attention, I learn from my mistakes, and I care more than I know how to explain.

So if I seem lost sometimes, please don't mistake it for a lack of feelings. I'm just someone who's learning how to love you properly, one little thing at a time.

honestly yours, Still trying ♡

page three

You are free

You belong to no one, but there's a relationship only between us — and it means nothing to other men.

Anyone can fall in love with you, and they are free to ask you out. You should decide who you like more.

If I'm not good enough, you can choose someone else.

I just hope that whoever you choose — even if it's not me — treats you the way you deserve to be treated. With patience, with care, and with the kind of love that makes you feel safe.

But for now, while you're still mine, I'll keep trying. I'll keep learning. And I'll keep being grateful for every little moment you give me.

always, Yours (for now & always) ♡
